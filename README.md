**<center> Segmentation Study </center>**

>A study comparing segmentation models on different crops, genotypes and vegetative development

# Objectives

The main objective of this study is to compare the different segmentation models' outputs on corn ears, sorghum panicles and wheat spikes field images. As minor objective we have questions to answer:

1. What is the perfomance of the models considering the different genotypes and collection dates on the different crops?
2. How much data is necessary to train a model - does augmentation really matters?
3. How are it going to be the metrics when training models in one collection date and infering in another?
4. How are going to be the metrics when training models in a bunch of genotype and infering in the others?
5. Does transfer learning from collection date to collection date impacts in the overall model performance?
